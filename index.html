  • 1975

    I was born in South Korea. My grandparents, parents, older sister and younger brother lived together. My grandparents are so lovely and kind. They were always good to me. I have beautiful memories with them.

  • 1981

    I entered an elementary school. I was so happy to be in school. And then I went to a middle school in my home town. And then I moved to a near city for a high school.

  • 1990

    I entered a mission school. And I met Jesus. Jesus has changed my whole life. I realized who I am. Jesus taught me how to live. I was also devoted to church activities.

  • 1993

    I entered university. However, I had to get a job for my tuition. It was not easy for me. The real world came to me. It was hard but sometimes fun. And I could find some possibilities for my future.

  • 2000

    Finally, I graduated university. Every healthy young Korean man should do military service. Meanwhile I did it and I got a bachelor degree.

  • 2003

    I met a beautiful woman. She was a pianist. She visited a church and played piano. I had crush on her at first. I kept contacting her and had dates.

  • 2005

    I proposed to the pianist. She accepted it. Finally, I got married with most beautiful woman in the world. And I realized that ‘to live happily ever after’ is not true. Another world came to me.

  • 2015

    I would tell that it’s only God’s grace. Frankly speaking, my wife and I haven’t had a plan for children. But God gave us children. Finally, my family was set. We have 3 sons and a daughter. And I was shocked that the last baby was a princess. Her brothers are monsters, but she is a princess. I don’t understand what happened. Maybe her mom must be a queen.

  • 2019

    My family moved to Canada. Like Abraham, my wife and I have departed without knowing where to go. All process took time but it was fine. It was hard for my family to say good bye to people including parents, friends and so on.

  • 2020

    I got some jobs in Canada, but I was not satisfied with them. God led me to study. Then I started to study computer programming for the second half of my life.
